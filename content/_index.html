Selecting Mutual Funds in Colombia

A lot of friends and colleagues usually ask me what they should do with their savings and where to invest them. This is not an easy question from a technical point of view and the answer depends on your risk aversion degree, your investment horizon and how much you have to invest, among other important variables. For that reason, I decided to tackle this question using information of all of the Colombian mutual funds available for the general public, using sifi data.

(Information provided here do not constitute any kind of financial/investment advice but is only an exercise for academic purposes)
